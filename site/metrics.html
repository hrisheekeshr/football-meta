Understanding the Numbers

Each metric provides insight into a player's performance but has limitations to consider. Below are brief explanations of some common statistics.

  • Completion Percentage: Measures a quarterback's accuracy, but can be inflated by short passes.
  • Yards After Catch: Highlights a receiver's ability to gain extra yards, though often influenced by play design.
  • Passer Rating Allowed: Gauges defensive backs, but doesn't fully account for defensive schemes.
  • Tackles: Simple to tally, yet doesn't differentiate between impactful plays and routine stops.

Context is key with any metric. Combine data points and consider game situations to form a complete picture.
